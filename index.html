About

About Me

My experience spans the for-profit, nonprofit and government sectors. In all of these, I've always found my way to data-centric roles, creating and interpreting systems that provide business-critical information to decision makers. Although my primary domains of expertise focus on CRM, HRMS, and financial systems, I've found opportunities to generalize my data and technology skills to scenarios ranging from nuclear regulatory compliance to selling tickets to Hamilton! I believe that this flexibility and capability to solve complex problems hinge on 2 essential factors:

  • Stretch yourself. Don't just think outside the box - think outside the galaxy.
  • Be inclusive. Engage with stakeholders in ways that encourage curiosity and create safe spaces for all.

In addition to my technical expertise, I have over 8 years of experience as a professional pianist, music director, arranger, synthesizer programmer, and music educator at the university level. For me, this background in arts and education is as essential to creative problem-solving as any technical skill. Although I'm not currently playing professionally, it's still one of my favorite things to do, especially if I'm stuck on a tech problem and need a new approach!
